FAQ

Quick answers.

How is this different from searching eBay sold listings myself?
Speed. Thrifteaz returns 8 sold comparables in 5 seconds vs ~10 minutes of manual research per item. If you scan 20 items a week, that's 3+ hours saved every week.
Is the AI accurate on designer pieces and vintage?
Multi-photo mode lets you scan the brand tag alongside the main photo for authentication-grade ID. Catches counterfeit Nike, Lululemon, fake vintage Levi's tags, designer hardware mismatches.
