Delete Your Thrifteaz Account

You can delete your Thrifteaz account and all associated data at any time, directly from the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Thrifteaz app on your phone
  2. Tap the Me tab at the bottom right
  3. Scroll down to Account
  4. Tap Delete account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

The deletion is immediate and irreversible. You'll be signed out and your account will be removed.

What gets deleted

  • Your account — email, sign-in credentials, profile
  • Scan history — all items you've scanned and AI identifications
  • Photos — all photos you've uploaded to Thrifteaz storage
  • Earnings tracker data — flip history and profit records
  • Community contributions — your validations and community activity
  • Voice profile — your AI-learned writing style
  • Subscription records — Pro subscription cancellation must be done separately via Google Play (subscriptions are not auto-cancelled by account deletion)

What may be retained

For legal and operational reasons, Thrifteaz may retain a limited amount of data after account deletion:

  • Anonymized usage events for product analytics (cannot be linked back to you)
  • Tax and transaction records as required by US law (typically retained for 7 years)
  • Crash logs and security event logs for up to 90 days

None of this retained data is linkable to your identity once your account is deleted.

Don't have access to the app?

If you can't sign in to delete your account through the app, email us at support@thrifteaz.com with the subject line "Account deletion request" and the email associated with your Thrifteaz account. We'll process your request within 7 business days.
